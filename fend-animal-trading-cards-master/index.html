Giant Panda

It can take up to 16 hours for a Panda to consume its daily serving of bamboo.

  • Scientific Name Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Height 2 to 3ft
  • Average Lifespan 20yrs
  • Habitat Remote mountain regions in China

The giant panda has a body shape typical of bears. It has black fur on its ears, eye patches, muzzle, legs, arms and shoulders. The rest of the animal's coat is white.
